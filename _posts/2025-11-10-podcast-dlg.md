I was really happy to be part of the DLG podcast about soil health and the ReGenfarm digital-twin work. The conversation felt like a great chance to bring together farmer experience, field measurements and models to talk about how we can better understand and manage soil processes at farm scale.
During the discussion, we talked about why soil health monitoring is so important today, and how a digital twin can bring together farmer knowledge, field data and models to turn complex soil processes into practical, actionable insights. We also touched on real-world monitoring strategies, the uncertainties and limitations of modelling, and how to translate scientific results into simple indicators that genuinely support on-farm decision-making.
